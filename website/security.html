How to report a vulnerability

Security First

One of the main goals of the Automotive Security Research Group is to share knowledge. But we understand that sharing security vulnerabilities in the open is undesirable in many ways for some security experts.

Therefore we hereby provide you with a way to help us research vulnerabilities, share them with our trusted partners and find solutions to address them or develop hardened proof of concepts.

Please use the following PGP key to encrypt your report and send us an email to security@asrg.io.
