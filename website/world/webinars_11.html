    Who: Gilad Bandel, Vice President of Products at Arilou Cyber Security

    Presentation:

    "CANbus vs. Ethernet IDS/IPS"

    A large diversity of attack scenarios can be launched over the CANbus that can exploit protocol inherent vulnerabilities and take advantage of the ECU software weaknesses. As previously demonstrated numerous times, those attacks that can be initiated from inside or outside the vehicle on compromised in-vehicle networks in such a manner which could jeopardize vehicle occupants, other road users and inflict damage to property. The presentation will describe a variety of attack scenarios on the CANbus itself, in various network topologies, explain the methods available to defend the vehicle in terms of detection and, wherever possible, prevention of the attack. The location of the Intrusion Detection System (IDS) and Intrusion Prevention System (IPS) will be discussed and various reporting mechanisms will be considered.
